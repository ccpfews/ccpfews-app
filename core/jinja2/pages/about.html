About CCPFEWS

Innovative Research Center in FEWS Nexus.

The Center for Cyber-Physical Food, Energy and Water system (CCPFEWS) is an entity of the University of Johannesburg.

CCPFEWS comprises a unique team of experienced, industrial and academic experts in Food, Energy, Water Systems (FEWS) and Cyber-physical spaces, providing innovative research and intellectual outputs.
